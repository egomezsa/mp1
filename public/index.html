Slothn is a group of relaxed programmers. We believe that the product's quality is directly correlated to the developer's happiness and reversely proportional to their stress. So our company's motto is chill till it happens.

Many corporations disagree with our approach because they staunchly believe that throughput is what keeps the company running. But that's their opinion, so whatever.

Every project is like a mountain. We like to slowly climb to the top of it and enjoy the view. When we are at the top, we like the thrill of rolling down.
Company policy dictates that every developer must take a beach break once every three weeks. We deeply believe that tanning is integral for the development cycle.
Sloths are Slothn's spirit animal. They represent relaxation and survival against everyone's expectations.
